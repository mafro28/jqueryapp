Healthy Living continues to inform and advise the public and a host of professionals including policy makers, academics, businesses and local councils on health-related issues in an attempt to bridge the gap between scientific research findings, policy and practice.


Information on a number of health related issues looking at topics such as health inequalities, the food we eat, what we are doing to tackle the obesity problem, the relationship between a person’s health and the work that they do as well as the relationship between the environment and human health.


Reducing screen time also contributes to a health living environment.
